##Overview
This data details City of San Jose city limits boundary.  It is updated monthly from the City's GIS database.

## Metadata
###Attribute Fields
`INCORP_ID` Incorporated ID  
`INCORP` Incorporated city name  
`AREA_SQ_MI` Area of San Jose in square miles  

### Projections
* GeoJSON, and KMZ files are in WGS84 coordinate system.
* The zipped files are shapefiles in NAD_1983_StatePlane_California_III_FIPS_0403_Feet projected coordinate system.
